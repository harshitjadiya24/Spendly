How to Use Spendly

1

Create your account

Sign up in seconds with your email and a strong password. No credit card needed — start tracking for free right away. Your data stays private.

2

Add expenses to accounts

Log income or expenses with category, amount, and date. Link each transaction to a wallet or bank account, and switch between INR, USD, EUR, and more. Scan receipt images to auto-fill fields.

3

Set budgets, goals & alerts

Create monthly budgets per category with optional rollover for unused amounts. Track savings goals with progress bars, and get alerts when budgets are near limits or bills are due.

4

Track loans, splits & investments

Record loans given or taken with EMI tracking. Split expenses with friends and settle up later. Monitor mutual funds, FDs, and stocks with real-time P&L — all in one dashboard.

5

Auto-categorize with rules

Set up categorization rules so recurring expenses like "Swiggy" or "Netflix" are automatically tagged. Imported CSV transactions from GPay, PhonePe, or your bank get categorized instantly.

6

Review, forecast & export

Browse your full ledger with filters by account, category, or date. Check your 30-day cash flow forecast, financial health score, and recurring bills. Export to CSV anytime for tax filing.
