Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account and using the App, including your name, email address, and expense data. We also collect basic usage data such as page views and feature interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the App — to log your expenses, generate insights, and personalise your experience. We never sell your personal information.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third parties except when required by law or when necessary to operate the App (e.g., cloud hosting providers). Any third party we work with is bound by strict confidentiality terms.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account and data at any time. You can export your data or request its permanent deletion by contacting our support team.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain session state. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the App.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
